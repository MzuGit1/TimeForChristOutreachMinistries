Church Year Plan

This page outlines the annual ministry focus, events, and programmes for Time For Christ Outreach Ministries. It helps members stay aligned with the vision and direction of the church throughout the year.

Quarter 1: Foundation & Prayer

  • 21 Days of Prayer & Fasting
  • Leadership Alignment Meeting
  • Youth Kick-Off Service
  • Community Outreach Launch

Quarter 2: Growth & Discipleship

  • Discipleship Classes
  • Women’s Conference
  • Men’s Fellowship Weekend
  • Sunday School Teacher Training

Quarter 3: Evangelism & Impact

  • Evangelism Month
  • Youth Revival Weekend
  • Community Feeding Programme
  • Hospital & Prison Ministry Visits

Quarter 4: Celebration & Thanksgiving

  • Thanksgiving Service
  • Year-End Celebration
  • Leaders Appreciation Day
  • Christmas Outreach

Monthly Focus Themes

  • January: Prayer & Dedication
  • February: Love & Family
  • March: Faith & Fasting
  • April: Resurrection Power
  • May: Spiritual Growth
  • June: Youth Empowerment
  • July: Community Service
  • August: Women of Purpose
  • September: Men of Strength
  • October: Evangelism
  • November: Thanksgiving
  • December: Celebration
