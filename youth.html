Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry of Time For Christ Outreach Ministries is committed to raising bold, passionate, and spiritually grounded young people who live for Christ and influence their world.

Our Vision

To empower young people to discover their identity in Christ, grow in faith, and walk in purpose.

Our Mission

To build a vibrant youth community through worship, teaching, mentorship, creativity, and impactful outreach.

Message from the Youth Leader

“This is where the Youth Leader can share a message of encouragement, purpose, and direction for all young people in the ministry.”
