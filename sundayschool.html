Sunday School (Children’s Ministry)

The Sunday School Ministry of Time For Christ Outreach Ministries is dedicated to teaching children the Word of God in a fun, safe, and loving environment. We believe in raising children who know Jesus, love Jesus, and walk in His ways.

Our Vision

To nurture children in the knowledge of Christ and help them grow in faith, character, and love.

Our Mission

To teach biblical truths in a child‑friendly way through stories, songs, activities, and practical lessons that build strong spiritual foundations.

Message from the Sunday School Coordinator

“This is where the Sunday School Coordinator can share a message of love, encouragement, and guidance for parents and children.”

Curriculum Overview

  • Bible Stories & Lessons
  • Memory Verses
  • Worship & Songs
  • Creative Activities (drawing, crafts, drama)
  • Character Building Lessons
  • Prayer & Devotion Time

Activities & Programmes

  • Children’s Sunday Services
  • Holiday Bible School
  • Children’s Choir
  • Kids Fun Day
  • Parent–Child Workshops
