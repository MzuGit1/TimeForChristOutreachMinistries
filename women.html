Women’s Ministry

The Women’s Ministry of T4C Outreach Ministries is dedicated to empowering women spiritually, emotionally, and socially. We believe in raising women of purpose, strength, and godly character.

Our Vision

To build women who are rooted in Christ, walking in purpose, and impacting their families, church, and community with excellence.

Our Mission

To disciple, equip, and support women through fellowship, prayer, teaching, mentorship, and community service.

Message from the Women’s Ministry Leader

“This is where the Women’s Ministry Leader can share a message of encouragement, purpose, and direction for all women in the ministry.”
