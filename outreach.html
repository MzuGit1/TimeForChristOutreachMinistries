Community Outreach Programmes

Time For Christ Outreach Ministries is committed to transforming communities through practical support, compassion, and the love of Christ. Our outreach programmes serve families, youth, the elderly, and vulnerable groups across our community.

Our Outreach Vision

To be a beacon of hope, restoration, and support in our community by meeting physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.

Food Security & Poverty Relief

  • Weekly food parcel distribution
  • Emergency food support for families in crisis
  • Community feeding days for children and the elderly

Youth Development & Education Support

  • After‑school tutoring
  • Mentorship and life‑skills programmes
  • Safe recreational activities

Skills Development & Empowerment

  • Digital literacy training
  • CV writing and job‑readiness workshops
  • Entrepreneurship support

Family & Social Support

  • Counselling and emotional support
  • Substance‑abuse intervention referrals
  • Support for victims of trauma

Community Events & Social Cohesion

  • Youth events and holiday programmes
  • Community clean‑up campaigns
  • Health awareness days

Volunteer With Us

We welcome volunteers who want to make a difference. Whether you can help with food distribution, tutoring, administration, or events — there is a place for you.
