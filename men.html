Men’s Ministry

The Men’s Ministry of Time For Christ Outreach Ministries is committed to raising strong, disciplined, and spiritually mature men who lead their families, church, and community with integrity and purpose.

Our Vision

To build men who are rooted in Christ, walking in strength, leadership, and godly character.

Our Mission

To equip men through teaching, fellowship, mentorship, and service, empowering them to fulfill their God-given roles as leaders and protectors.

Message from the Men’s Ministry Leader

“This is where the Men’s Ministry Leader can share a message of strength, encouragement, and direction for all men in the ministry.”
